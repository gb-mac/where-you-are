# The Common — Introduction Scene

*Two paths in. Same room. Different terms.*

---

## The Coin

You come back slowly. Ceiling first. Wood, old, water-stained in one corner. Warm. Somewhere that's been heated deliberately for a long time.

A woman is checking your dressing without looking at your face. She's done this before. When she's satisfied she steps back and makes a note.

*"You're at The Common. You've been out about six hours. Nothing that won't close."*

She moves to the door, then pauses.

*"The coin's on the table. Keeper held it for you."*

You look. Gold. Warden stamp. The one you found three weeks ago in a coat pocket at the salvage site. You kept it without knowing why.

*"That's what got you in."*

She leaves. The coin is on the table. The room is warm. Outside, very faintly, you can hear other people moving through the building — someone eating, someone talking low, a door closing somewhere down the hall.

You're inside something. You didn't know it existed until you needed it.

---

## The Debt

You come back slowly. Ceiling first. Wood, old, water-stained in one corner. Warm. Somewhere that's been heated deliberately for a long time.

A woman is checking your dressing without looking at your face. When she's satisfied she steps back and makes a note.

*"You're at The Common. You've been out about six hours. Nothing that won't close."*

She moves to the door. A man is standing there you didn't notice — standing the way people stand when they've been waiting and they're used to waiting.

He looks at you for a moment. Not unkind.

*"Someone saw you go down near the door. We brought you in."*

A beat.

*"You don't have the coin."*

Not an accusation. A fact he's establishing before the next part.

*"When you're on your feet, come find me. We'll figure out what's fair."*

He leaves. The doctor leaves. The room is warm. Outside, very faintly, you can hear other people moving through the building.

You're inside something. You owe it.

---

*Both players are in The Common now. The coin player has their coin back and no obligations. The debt player has a Keeper who made a call about them when they couldn't make it themselves, and a favour that needs paying.*

*The debt player's story is longer. The Keeper knows their name before they know the Keeper's.*
