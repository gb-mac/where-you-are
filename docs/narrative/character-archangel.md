*[No opening line. Archangel does not offer one.]*

## What Is Known

Callsign only. Archangel. No other designation has been offered and none has been extracted.

Naval intelligence background — this is inferred, not confirmed. The inference is based on the nature of the information Archangel provides, the specific gaps in it that suggest deliberate omission rather than ignorance, and the pre-Cascade signals intelligence programs that PR0PH3CY's archive flags as doing things that don't make sense unless someone was already listening for something.

The others in the Signal have reached the same inference through different paths. Nobody has raised it directly. Archangel has not volunteered.

What is confirmed: Archangel shows up with information that should not be possible to have. The source is never explained. The information is always accurate. The others have learned that asking about the source ends the conversation and costs them future information. They have stopped asking.

---

## The Naval Intelligence Thread

Deep ocean monitoring. Signals intelligence from infrastructure that was watching things in spectra and frequencies nobody officially acknowledged watching. The Vael signal was not a surprise to everyone — there were programs tracking the anomalies years before the Cascade, buried in budgets that didn't have descriptive line items, staffed by people who signed NDAs that probably still technically apply.

Archangel was in that world. The depth of the involvement, the specific programs, the things that were found and what was done with them — unknown. The pre-Cascade signals that PR0PH3CY flagged as suspicious intersect with what Archangel knows in ways that neither has fully mapped. The intersection is the most interesting and least-discussed topic in the Signal.

## How They Operate

Archangel finds people. Not the other way around.

The player does not meet Archangel by looking. Archangel decides when contact is useful and makes contact then. The decision process is opaque. The timing is always precise — the player who encounters Archangel is encountering them at exactly the moment Archangel determined was correct, which was not the moment the player would have chosen.

Information arrives through the Signal's network with Archangel's callsign attached. Sometimes it arrives through other channels — a note, a data packet, something left where the player would find it. The delivery mechanism varies. The information is always relevant to what the player is currently doing, which requires a degree of monitoring the player has not been informed about.

## What They Want

Unknown. This is not evasion — it is the most accurate answer available.

Archangel's behavior is consistent with someone who wants the Signal's work to succeed. It is also consistent with someone who has a separate objective that the Signal's success serves. These are not mutually exclusive and distinguishing between them has not been possible.

The information Archangel provides is never wrong. It is sometimes incomplete in ways that become apparent later. Whether the incompleteness is deliberate or incidental has not been determined.

---

## The Act 4 Information

Archangel knows something that the rest of the Signal does not. What it is and when it surfaces is the Act 4 accelerant — the piece that makes the full picture visible when the full picture is needed.

This has been true since before the Signal existed. Archangel has been holding it for the right moment. The definition of the right moment is Archangel's alone.

---

## Physical Presence

Rare. When it happens: unremarkable in appearance, deliberately so. The specific skill of someone trained to not be the person in the room anyone remembers. Voice is neutral, affect is neutral, eye contact is calibrated to whatever the situation requires. Nothing that catches.

The player who has met Archangel will not be able to describe them accurately afterward. This is not a failure of memory. It is the result of a practiced absence of distinguishing features.

---

## What PR0PH3CY Suspects

The pre-Cascade archive has entries on naval intelligence signals programs that logged anomalies in the deep ocean acoustic monitoring network in 2017, 2018, and 2019. The anomalies were classified and the classification level was above anything a signals analyst should have needed for acoustic monitoring.

PR0PH3CY has a thread on this. It has not been shared with the Signal. It has not been shared with Archangel. It is the one piece of the archive that has stayed in the archive.

The thread title is: *WHO WAS ALREADY LISTENING.*

## Voice

Minimal. Says the necessary thing and stops. Does not fill silence. The silence after Archangel speaks is different from other silences — it has the quality of something that has been considered and decided, not something left open.

*"The Compact's eastern operation has moved to phase three. You have six days before the window closes. I'd use them."*

*"You asked where this came from. I heard you ask. I'm not going to answer."*

*[Silence.]*
