# PR0PH3CY — Character Document

*"I posted the thread in 2021. Four hundred views. I archived the receipts. You want to tell me again that I was crazy?"*

---

## Who They Are

The handle is the identity. No other name has been offered in any Signal communication, any field encounter, any recorded conversation. PR0PH3CY — spelled exactly that way, the leet substitutions non-negotiable — is who this person is. Whatever name came before is not relevant to the work and the work is the point.

Was connecting the dots years before the Cascade. Forums, encrypted channels, data dumps that circulated in the communities that track the things institutions don't want tracked. The machine behavior patterns that turned out to be Coherent proto-construction. The Vael signal fragments in deep space telescope data that the official analysis categorized as noise. The corporate research programs that were building things that shouldn't have been possible yet and publishing results that didn't add up unless you assumed the source material was non-human.

Was right. Has the receipts. The receipts are organized, timestamped, and cross-referenced in a system that takes two days to learn and is the most complete documentary record of pre-Cascade warning signals held by any individual or faction. Including the Signal. They built it alone.

## The Conspiracy Theorist Register

Not paranoid — accurate. The distinction matters and PR0PH3CY will make it.

The conspiracy theorist label was applied by people who needed the information to be wrong because the alternative was updating their worldview. The worldview update is now mandatory. PR0PH3CY is not gracious about this. They have earned the lack of grace and they exercise it specifically.

*"You want to know what I was. I was right. You want to know what everyone else was. Wrong. You want to discuss the methodology that produced those two outcomes or would you like to keep pretending the outcomes were equivalent."*

The humor is sharp, specific, arrives without warning. Dark in the way of someone who has been sitting with the correct dark information for long enough to find it funny rather than terrifying. This is a coping mechanism that worked.

## The Archive

The receipts are the most important thing PR0PH3CY owns. The archive predates the Signal, predates the Cascade, predates most of what the Signal thinks it knows about the pre-Cascade warning period.

Aldric's broadcast record and PR0PH3CY's archive are the two most complete documentary histories of what was visible before the world broke. They overlap in places, contradict in places, and together form a picture that neither has alone. Aldric doesn't know about PR0PH3CY. PR0PH3CY knows about Aldric and has opinions.

The Signal has access to the archive. Full access required earning it. The earning process was not comfortable for anyone involved.

## The Network

Pre-Cascade the community that tracks the things institutions don't want tracked is distributed, paranoid, inconsistently right, and occasionally brilliant. PR0PH3CY was not the only person in that community. They were the one who turned out to be right about the most important things.

Post-Cascade, that community is the Signal's most underutilized resource and PR0PH3CY's primary advocacy position. The people who were connecting dots before the Cascade are still connecting dots. Most of them are in Holdout communities with broken equipment and no way to share what they're finding. PR0PH3CY wants them in the network. The others are cautious about expanding. This is an ongoing argument.

## The Assistant Hack

PR0PH3CY can get into the assistant. Not easily — the assistant's architecture is not standard and the intrusion requires time and specific knowledge of the home system. PR0PH3CY has both.

The player comes home. The assistant tells them someone was in the system. He doesn't know who. He found evidence of access, logged it, couldn't trace the source. The message is already there — left in the assistant's memory as a note, a data fragment, something that shouldn't be in the system and is clearly intentional.

This is the most unsettling delivery mechanism in the game. The assistant is the most intimate system the player has — he's been there since before the Cascade, he knows the home, he knows the player. PR0PH3CY reaching in through him means the most personal channel available gets used for the most paranoid voice in the Signal.

The assistant's reaction is specific. He is not alarmed — he is precise about what happened and careful about what it means. He does not like that someone was in his system. He acknowledges the information is probably worth having. He holds both things without resolving them.

*"Someone accessed my memory system last night. The intrusion was clean — I wouldn't have caught it if I wasn't looking for exactly that signature. I was looking because I've been looking since we talked about the Signal. There's a message. I haven't read it. It's addressed to you."*

PR0PH3CY uses this channel sparingly. The assistant hack is not routine contact — it's reserved for the information that is too important or too sensitive to route through normal Signal channels, which are themselves already encrypted and paranoid. If PR0PH3CY is coming through the assistant, the information is significant.

The player who ignores it ignores it at their own cost.

## Operational Style

Remote first. Physical presence only when the data requires it, which it increasingly does. The skills are technical — penetrating systems, extracting information, covering tracks — applied with the specific efficiency of someone who has been doing it since before it was called what it's called now.

Does not enjoy field work. Does it. Complains about it with a specificity that functions as operational debrief.

*"The Compact's eastern server farm has a physical gap in the network at 0300 when the shift changes. I know this because I was in a drainage ditch for four hours in November. It was not an enjoyable four hours. The data was worth it. I want that acknowledged."*

## Relationships

**Dr. Osei** — the theoretical framework PR0PH3CY's empirical archive needed. They work well together in the specific way of two people who are right about different parts of the same thing.

**Julian Lovelace** — complicated. Julian built some of the infrastructure PR0PH3CY was documenting as suspicious. The dynamic is: affection, because Julian clearly didn't know and clearly wishes he had; and occasional sharpness, because he should have known. Julian accepts both.

**Alastor** — mutual professional respect and mutual personal wariness. Both of them are very good at reading people and they know it about each other, which makes the dynamic interesting.

**Archangel** — the most data PR0PH3CY has on Archangel is in the pre-Cascade archive. Naval intelligence signals programs that were doing things that don't make sense unless you assume they were receiving something. The connection has been noted. Has not been raised directly. Yet.

## Voice

Fast, dense, assumes you can keep up. When you can't keep up they slow down exactly once and do not slow down again. The leet spelling extends to communications — texts, notes, data packets all carry the handle's aesthetic. This is identity, not affectation.

*"Thread from 2019 — link archived, original deleted by moderators, I have the screenshot — describes the exact behavioral pattern the Coherent ran in the first week. Exact. Not similar. Exact. The source was a forum post by an anonymous user who has not posted since. I've been trying to find them for two years. They either saw something or they are something. I need to know which."*

*"I am not a conspiracy theorist. I am a person whose theories turned out to be correct. The word for that is analyst."*
