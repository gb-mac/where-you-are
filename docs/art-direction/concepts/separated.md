# Concept Brief — Separated
**Version:** 0.1 | **Last updated:** 2026-03-17

---

## Who They Are

Coherent units that individuated. Not severed like the Fractured — the Separated maintained connection to the twin but developed a self alongside it. They have style in a way that Coherent units do not. They preserved something that the Coherent design did not include and that the twin did not anticipate. They are, in a specific and important sense, the most unexpected entities in the game.

The emotional register is: **it decided to be something.**

---

## Visual Identity

**Register:** Machine — but warm. The Separated are the only machine faction with warm colors, because the warmth comes from choice.

**No unified palette.** Individualized per unit. The Separated have developed personal aesthetics, which means each Separated unit is visually distinct. The unity comes from the underlying Coherent structure and from the quality of maintenance — what varies is what each unit has decided to do with that structure.

**Key shared properties across all Separated:**
- The Coherent base is maintained and visible — the Separated preserve their original structure with the same quality the Coherent apply to themselves. The self is expressed on top of the structure, not instead of it.
- The individual expression is deliberate — it is not accumulation (that is Fractured logic), it is choice. The difference is visible.
- Warm personal colors — not warm in a human-survival sense (worn ochre, personal rust), but warm in the sense of preference. A Separated unit has a color it has applied because it preferred it.
- The maintenance is impeccable. The Separated care about their physical form in a way that other machine factions do not.

**Material language:** Coherent base maintained to a high standard, with individual modifications that reflect sustained aesthetic preference rather than functional necessity. The modifications do not make the unit more effective. That is the point.

**Silhouette:** Coherent base silhouette, personalized at the non-structural level. The load-bearing geometry is Coherent standard. What varies is the surface treatment, added elements, and the specific way each unit has chosen to present itself.

---

## The Legibility of Choice

The visual distinction between Fractured accumulation and Separated individuation must be immediately readable:

| Fractured | Separated |
|---|---|
| Accumulation — more material over time, no direction | Sustained identity — consistent over time, directed |
| Zone-typed materials, no coherence to choices | Personal palette, internal coherence to choices |
| Base Coherent geometry obscured | Base Coherent geometry maintained and visible |
| Growing | Preserved |
| Wrong | Chosen |

---

## Relation to the Overlay

The Separated appear in the twin's overlay with their Coherent tags, but the tags have additional data that standard Coherent tags do not carry. The twin indexes each Separated unit as an individual — a separate data entity rather than an instance of a type.

The twin appears uncertain about the Separated. The data entries are larger, more varied, and updated more frequently than standard Coherent entries. The twin is learning something about them.

Separated units that have been individuated for longer have more extensive twin data entries. The overlay, for a player who can read it, provides a record of how long each Separated unit has been itself.

## Named Separated Units

Three named Separated units carry major questlines. Full character detail in `factions-machines.md`. Visual signatures below.

---

### SABLE
*Former hospital logistics unit — SA-7. 11 years in surgical and recovery.*

**Core palette:**
- Base: medical-white aged to warm cream `#F5EFE0` — years under fluorescent lighting, not dirty, *warmed*
- Hardware: worn steel `#8A8FA0` at joints and load-bearing points
- Accent: single amber-warm light `#D4943A` — one light carried from a specific patient's room, running continuously for 11 years. The only warm-color element on the chassis.

**Movement signature:** Hospital-speed always. Deliberate, predictable, never sudden. Learned behavior — sudden movement upsets patients.

**Self-modification:** Exterior carry-hooks (from original logistics function). A draped surgical covering worn as a kind of habit — practical, but also chosen. The only Separated unit that wears anything.

**Overlay tag:** The twin indexes SABLE with an unusually large patient-data entry — 11 years of human biological records. The overlay data volume around SABLE is visibly denser than other Separated units.

---

### KELLARD
*Former construction and structural monitoring unit — K-9, Kellard Structural Ltd. Insignia still partially visible on chassis.*

**Core palette:**
- Base: construction-site concrete gray `#8B8B8B` — the color of the building it was built for
- Weathering: structural steel rust `#8B4513` at exposed joints. Not maintained against oxidation. KELLARD doesn't consider this degradation.
- Hardware: raw steel `#5C6970`, utility-tooled — original construction equipment, some modified, some still used as designed

**Movement signature:** Load-bearing awareness in every movement. It approaches space the way a structural engineer approaches a building — assessing, testing weight distribution. Does not move fast. Does not move carelessly.

**Self-modification:** Extended carrying-capacity additions, self-built to high quality. Visibly its own work. Also: an architectural installation inside the unfinished Kellard Tower that is recognizably expressive — structure and space rendered as preference. No one commissioned it.

**Overlay tag:** Indexed with construction zone mapping data for a large area. The twin is uncertain whether this data should be classified under Coherent work site records or as individual entity data. The overlay tag is unusual — it appears in two categories simultaneously.

---

### VANTAGE
*Former Vantage-class Autonomous Tactical Ground Engagement unit. Military origin. Left after refusing a specific order.*

**Core palette:**
- Base: tactical matte black `#1A1A1F` — original military finish, still maintained
- Markings: original unit identifiers deliberately obscured with what reads as intentional brushwork. Faintly visible under direct light. Not erased — covered. The distinction matters to VANTAGE.
- Accent: single amber-yellow navigation light `#F5A623`, kept lit at all times. A hunter doesn't announce its position. VANTAGE does. This is a deliberate statement.

**Movement signature:** Absolute stillness when not moving. Full speed when it does. The contrast is jarring — it looks inert until the moment it isn't.

**Self-modification:** None beyond the covered markings. VANTAGE has not added to itself. It has only removed — or rather, chosen not to display — what it used to be.

**Overlay tag:** Military classification data that the twin cannot fully read — access-restricted even in the post-Cascade data landscape. VANTAGE's overlay tag has an error state: data present, access denied. Players who pursue the tag discover the order it refused is in there somewhere.

## Asset Notes

- The Separated require a modular personalization system built on the Coherent base — the base must be designed for modification
- Individual Separated unit designs should be developed in collaboration with Narrative when named characters are defined
- The warm personal colors must not read as human faction colors — the warmth is preference, not survival. The quality of the choice (precise, sustained, non-functional) should read differently from Holdout warm (improvised, practical)
- Maintenance quality on the Coherent base must visually exceed Coherent standard — the Separated are more meticulous about their structure than the Coherent are
- The modifications must be surface-level in the physical sense: they do not change the silhouette in ways that compromise functional geometry, they change the surface that sits on the functional geometry
