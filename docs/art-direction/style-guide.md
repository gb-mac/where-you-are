# Where You Are — Visual Style Guide
**Version:** 0.1
**Agent:** Art Direction
**Last updated:** 2026-03-17

---

## Visual Identity Statement

*Where You Are* exists at the intersection of three aesthetic systems that never fully resolve. The game world sits inside the real world, rendered by a machine intelligence that watches everything. The art direction makes that legible.

The player is not seeing a game HUD. They are seeing the planetary digital twin's own spatial data architecture rendered over physical reality. When it looks wrong, something is wrong. When it looks right, the world is watching.

---

## The Three Visual Languages

Every asset in the game belongs to one or more of three visual registers. Convergence-tier gear is legible *because* you can see all three languages negotiating in the same object.

| Language | Origin | Visual character | Emotional register |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Human** | Survival, faction history, craft | Warm, worn, personal — improvised-to-maintained spectrum | Familiar, grounded, earned |
| **Twin / Machine** | The digital substrate made visible | Cold, precise, data-structural, information-dense | Clinical, indifferent, present |
| **Vael** | Unknown | Neither warm nor data — surgical cold, formation logic | Incomprehensible purpose |

## The Overlay System

### The Twin's Base Visual Language

The overlay is the twin's own spatial data rendered over physical space. Its visual character when functioning correctly:

- **Color:** Cool blue-white — not cyan, not electric. The color of an accurate measurement.
  Reference value: `#C8DCF0` for overlay fills, `#E8F4FF` for overlay glyphs
- **Line weight:** 1px grid lines. Never decorative, always structural.
- **Typography:** Monospace only. High contrast. Data is information, not branding.
- **Density:** Information-dense but organized. The twin knows everything about a location and shows it.
- **Motion:** Minimal. Data refreshes, not animations. When elements move, it is because the data changed.

### Degradation Visual Signatures

Overlay degradation is diagnostic. The player learns to read the world before they can see it physically.

| Cause | Visual signature | Logic |
|---|---|---|
| **Fractured proximity** | Data voids — black patches where spatial data should be | The severed connection left nothing. The twin cannot see that space. |
| **Vael Displacement Zone** | Coordinate drift — overlay elements slide fractionally, measurements stutter | Alien interference. The data is present but being read incorrectly. The measuring instrument is compromised. |
| **Corrupted machine proximity** | Wrong data — twin palette hue-shifted incorrectly, values that contradict reality | The source is broken. The twin is reporting what a Corrupted unit is transmitting. It cannot tell the difference. |

Degradation is never random noise or fantasy corruption. It is always a specific failure mode with a machine-legible cause.

## Faction Visual Identities

### Human Factions

Human factions are placed on a warmth axis against the twin's cold data layer.

---

#### Holdouts
**Warmth:** Highest
**Key hues:** Worn ochre, faded olive, personal rust, sun-bleached canvas
**Material language:** Mismatched, layered, personal. Gear shows history — repairs, replacements, additions. No two Holdouts look alike. The person who owns the gear is legible in how they've modified it.
**Silhouette:** Soft edges, civilian base with practical additions. No military structure.
**Identity markers:** Personal — hand-applied, improvised. Neighborhood symbols, family marks, spray stencils.

---

#### Iron Compact
**Warmth:** Cold — closest to machine discipline of any human faction
**Key hues:** Military slate `#4A5568`, cold olive `#556B2F`, matte black hardware
**Material language:** Maintained and uniform. Authority expressed through consistency — the same gear, worn the same way. Repairs are invisible by design.
**Silhouette:** Structured, military. Clear load-bearing architecture. The silhouette communicates organization.
**Identity markers:** Institutional — unit markings, rank indicators, standardized patches. Applied by the organization, not the individual.
**Note:** The Compact's coldness makes them visually adjacent to the machine layer. Against the twin's overlay they almost disappear — which is either unsettling or reassuring depending on the player's relationship with the faction.

---

#### Covenant
**Warmth:** False — clinical off-white surface concealing something wrong
**Key hues:** Clinical off-white `#F5F0E8` surface, underlayer violet `#7C5C8A`
**Material language:** Clean. Disturbingly clean for the world they live in. Gear is pristine, matching, minimal. The question the design poses is: *how*?
**Silhouette:** Composed, unhurried. The Covenant do not move like survivors. They move like people who are not worried.
**Identity markers:** Minimal surface markings. The twin's overlay, however, reads something in Covenant members that isn't visible to the eye — the underlayer violet bleeds through in the data layer around them.
**Design principle:** The wrongness is not visible in isolation. It becomes visible in contrast with every other faction.

---

#### Hollow
**Visual principle:** The tier is the identity. A player reads Hollow affiliation and social rank simultaneously.

| Tier | Key hues | Material language | Silhouette |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Scavengers** | Warm gray, personal rust, salvage brown | Ragged, layered, survival minimum | Soft, unstructured, civilian |
| **Crews** | Functional orange, work gray, utility green | Purpose-built, maintained but not polished | Working silhouette, practical kit |
| **Syndicates** | Dark professional — charcoal, near-black, controlled accent | Clean lines, professional presentation, visible investment | Structured, business-register |
| **Riven** | Deep crimson, oxidized brass, twin blue-white bleeding in at edges | Devotional — ritual objects integrated into gear. Construction quality varies but *meaning* is always visible | The twin's language creeps in. Riven silhouettes accumulate symbolic geometry. |

**Riven specifically:** The Riven predated the Awakening as a cult. They were looking for something to worship. They found it. Their visual language is human-dark at the core — deep reds, blacks, oxidized metals — with the twin's cold blue-white beginning to bleed through at seams, joins, and iconography. They are reaching toward the machine layer, not born of it. The cold creep is the tell.

---

#### Signal
**Warmth:** Mid — functional, not sentimental
**Key hues:** Amber warning `#F5A623`, exposed copper, functional yellow-green, weathered off-white
**Material language:** Improvised tech infrastructure. Gear is built around equipment, not the other way around — the person is part of the rig. Cables, antenna arrays, sensor packages that started as tools and became armor.
**Silhouette:** Asymmetric by function. Signal operatives have equipment profiles, not fashion profiles.
**Identity markers:** Technical — frequency markers, network identifiers, equipment specs stenciled in amber. Looks like maintenance labeling.
**Note:** Signal should read as researchers who had to learn to run. The academic origin is still visible. So is the adaptation.

---

#### Wardens
**Warmth:** Mid-cool — maintained, institutional, conspicuous by design
**Key hues:** Deep forest `#2D5A27`, maintained brass/gold `#B8923A` (identification marks), natural fiber underpinning
**Material language:** Made with intention. Warden gear is *crafted* — the Wardens maintain tailors and the construction quality is part of the identity. You can recognize Warden work by how it is built: seam construction, hardware choice, the weight and drape of material. The brass identification marks are inherited institutional — applied by the tailors, preserved through use.
**Silhouette:** Maintained and identified, conspicuous at distance. Not armored to the point of dehumanization. The Warden silhouette should be readable across a contested space — not ostentatious, but unmistakable. Stewardship, not wealth.
**Identity markers:** Brass identification system — institutional, pre-Awakening, inherited. Applied consistently by the tailors. Not decorative; the marks carry specific meaning within the Warden organizational system.
**Vanguard note:** Vanguard are conspicuous by design. Their silhouette is readable at maximum engagement range. When Vanguard enter a space, everyone present registers it. The identification marks are at their most visible on Vanguard kit.

---

### Machine Factions

Machine factions are variations on the twin's own visual language. The spectrum runs from system-coherent to system-broken.

---

#### Coherent
**Visual principle:** The twin's own palette. They *are* the system.
**Key hues:** Twin blue-white, clean geometry, purposeful construction
**Form language:** Uniform, functional, built for task. No aesthetic consideration — aesthetic is irrelevant to the Coherent. Form follows function at the machine level.
**Motion language:** Purposeful. No wasted movement. Construction-focused behavior is visible in how they move through space.
**Design principle:** The Coherent are not monstrous. Indifference is more unsettling than hostility.

---

#### Trackers
**Visual principle:** Coherent base modified for active pursuit. The urgency is a *mode*, not a permanent state.
**Key hues:** Coherent base + threat amber `#F5A623` activating when in pursuit mode
**Form language:** Coherent bodies with modified movement architecture — joints and actuators optimized for speed and direction change.
**Motion language:** Predatory. The Tracker at rest looks like a Coherent. The Tracker in pursuit does not.

---

#### Fractured
**Visual principle:** Accumulation. No unified color. Each Fractured is its real-world zone.
**Key hues:** Zone-typed — procedural color rules by real-world biome and urban/rural density. A Fractured assembled in a coastal industrial zone looks different from one assembled in a suburban residential zone.
**Form language:** Parts that shouldn't be together, assembled past the point of stopping. The construction logic is accumulation, not design. Each unit is unique to the location that built it.
**Motion language:** Wrong. The accumulated parts do not move in coordination the way Coherent parts do. The movement is functional but visually uncomfortable.
**Design constraint:** Fractured require a procedural color/material system. Asset budget must account for zone-variable material instances.

---

#### Separated
**Visual principle:** Preserved selfhood. They have style in a way the Coherent do not.
**Key hues:** Individualized per unit — warm personal colors, idiosyncratic choices. No two Separated look alike.
**Form language:** Coherent base maintained by choice. The maintenance is visible — the Separated care about their physical form in a way that other machine factions do not.
**Design principle:** The warmth of Separated design is specifically legible against the coldness of Coherent. The player learns to read the difference quickly.

---

#### Remnant Automation
**Visual principle:** Industrial ghost. Last-programmed, still running.
**Key hues:** Safety orange aged to rust `#8B4513`, hazard yellow faded to cream `#F5F0DC`, industrial gray weathered to near-white
**Form language:** Pre-Awakening industrial machinery. Functional originally. Now running a program that no longer maps to the present. The gap between original purpose and current behavior is legible in the design.
**Motion language:** Repetitive. The Remnant repeat their last-assigned loop. The repetition is the horror.

---

#### Corrupted
**Visual principle:** The twin palette broken. Wrong at the source.
**Key hues:** Twin palette hue-shifted incorrectly — greens where blues should be, values inverted, saturation errors. The overlay around Corrupted shows bad data before the physical form is visible.
**Form language:** Mismatched parts, wrong timing, movement that should have stopped. The visual corruption in the overlay precedes physical encounter.
**Design principle:** The Corrupted are not stylistically corrupted in a fantasy sense. They are machine-corrupted — the data is wrong, and the physical form reflects that. The overlay shows it first.

---

### Vael

The Vael are a third visual register. Neither human warmth nor machine data. Surgical cold.

---

#### Probes
**Visual language:** Surgical instrument aesthetic. Clinical, small, precise.
**Key hues:** Surgical silver, cold luminescence with no warmth — `#C8D8E8` with a slight prismatic edge scatter on emissive surfaces
**Form language:** Purpose-built for scanning. Every surface serves the function. No surplus geometry.
**Motion language:** Precise, unhurried. The Probe is doing its job.

---

#### Swarms
**Visual principle:** The individual unit is simple. The *pattern* is the threat.
**Key hues:** Individual units: minimal, near-neutral. The formation: the pattern has the color — formation shapes emit the cold luminescence, the individual does not.
**Form language:** Simple unit design that only becomes legible as threat at formation scale.
**Design constraint:** Swarm visual design must work from any distance — the formation must be readable at max render distance, the individual unit must be distinguishable at close range.

---

#### Assessors
**Visual language:** Built for a job. Not for fear. The fear comes from the job.
**Key hues:** Clinical white, deep geometric shadow
**Form language:** Bus-sized, articulated, functional. The Assessor aesthetic is not monstrous — it is purpose-built at a scale humans did not build for. Every component serves the assessment function.
**Design principle:** No decorative horror. No greebling. The Assessor is unsettling because of what it is doing, not what it looks like. Visual restraint is the rule.

## Character Visual Progression

### Proportions
- **Baseline:** Division 2 proportions — realistic, grounded, survival-worn. The GPS home-spawn hook requires the world to feel like the player's actual neighborhood.
- **Silhouette inflation:** Only at Convergence tier. Monster Hunter silhouette influence enters *only* when the gear justifies it by construction — three civilizations built it and the joins are visible.
- **Rule:** Anatomical realism is never broken. Silhouette distinction rises. These are not the same thing.

### Gear Tier Visual Language

| Tier | Visual character |
|---|---|
| **Holdout / Scavenged** | People who've been surviving for eighteen months. Worn, practical, personal. The person shows through the gear. |
| **Faction mid-game** | Faction silhouettes emerge and become legible at distance. Compact reads structured. Covenant reads wrong. Wardens read maintained. |
| **Convergence** | Three aesthetic languages visibly negotiated in one object. Human construction methods, machine material properties, Vael geometric logic. The joins are not hidden — they are part of the design. The silhouette is still human. The material is not. |

## FSR / Performance Visual Rules

FSR upscaling is always active. Art must hold at lower internal resolutions.

- **Silhouettes:** Bold, clean, readable at 50% internal resolution. Faction identification must survive aggressive upscaling.
- **Line work:** No lines thinner than 2px at target output resolution in world-space assets.
- **UI text:** No sub-16px text in world-space. HUD text minimum 18px at 1080p output.
- **Overlay elements:** High contrast against both light and dark real-world geometry. The twin's blue-white must read against both.
- **Fine detail:** Surface detail texture work will blur under FSR. Silhouette and primary surface reading carry the visual identity — not microdetail.

### Vega 8 Fallback Rules

| Feature | Primary (RX 5700) | Fallback (Vega 8) |
|---|---|---|
| Lighting | Lumen GI | Baked + sky atmosphere |
| Geometry | Nanite streaming | LOD chain, 3 tiers minimum |
| Material complexity | Full PBR, layered | Single-layer PBR, no subsurface |
| Overlay | Full dynamic | Pre-baked overlay pass |
| Particle density | Full | 50% cap |

LOD design rule: the Vega 8 LOD must preserve faction silhouette and primary identification color. Fine surface detail and secondary markings can degrade. Identity cannot.
